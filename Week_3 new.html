INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY KHARAGPUR

Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur) is a public university established by the government of India in Kharagpur, West Bengal, India. Established in 1951, the institute is the first of the IITs to be established and is recognised as an Institute of National Importance.
